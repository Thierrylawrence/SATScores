About Page

Purpose: The context behind the project is that I wanted to see which states had a higher SAT score. I wanted to see if there was a correlation between big states and higher scores because there are more people, or maybe smaller states and higher scores because less of them take the exams. I am also just interested in the average score in general and whather or not someone's place of residency would impact it.
