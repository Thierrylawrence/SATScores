About Page

Purpose: The context behind the project is that I wanted to see which states had a higher SAT score. I wanted to see if there was a correlation between big states and higher scores because there are more people, or maybe smaller states and higher scores because less of them take the exams.

The feedback I received in my micro and macro prototypes were these:

  • 1. The photo had a shadow on it
  • 2. The color scheme for the map is bad because red and blue generally indicate the political parties, democrat and republican, when they are on a map of the USA. Even if it doesn't, then that is what people would assume when they saw it.
  • 3. Include Staten Island in the microprototype map
  • 4. Outline every state properly.
    • I will modify all of these things in the final version.

    *Note: The SAT is an optional exam for college entry. It is graded on a scale of 400 to 1600,in increments of 10, with the mean score being around 1080
